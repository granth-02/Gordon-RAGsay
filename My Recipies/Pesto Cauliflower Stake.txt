Name:  Pesto Cauliflower Stake 
Cuisine: Plant-Based
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins 
Cook TIme: 25 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Pesto
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Nutty, pesto flavour

Ingredients:
Cauliflower 1 piece
2 cups of my quick pesto
Mushroom 2-3 pieces
Garlic Cloves 2-3 piece
Cherry Tomatatoes 2-3 piece
Olive oil 3 tbsp
Italian Herbs 2 tbsp

Procedure:
In a pan add olive oil, garlic, italian seasoning 
Saute cherry tomatoes and mushroom till cooked well
Cut a slice of cauliflower such a way it’s a huge chunk and flat on both sides
Rub the cauliflower piece with olive oil and italian herbs
Add it into an air fryer for 15 minutes, on half way point flip the steak piece
In a serving dish add in the pesto sauce and veggies
Place the steak in the center, drizzle it with little olive oil

My Notes: Super fancy veg dish that I like to serve to impress. It is very simple to make and damn tasty. The dominant taste is of pesto and garlic as it is the main base, the mushroom contributes sweetness and nuttiness of the dish where cherry tomatoes pop with freshness. The steak it self is a great alternative to meat, and the cauliflower brings great earthy and nutty flavour
 
Alternatives:
Can add more veggies 
Can use tradional pesto to make it more authentic
Can use ready made pesto
