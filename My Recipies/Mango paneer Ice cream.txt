Name:  Mango paneer Ice cream 
Cuisine: Ice Cream
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 8 hours
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Mango
Main Flavour profile: Fruity, sweet and mango 

Ingredients:
Paneer 200g
Milk ¼ cup
Natural Mango pulp 200g
Diced Mango ⅛ cup

Procedure:
Blend paneer, milk, mango pulp in nutri blender
Ensure it is completely smooth
Pour it in a jar, and top it with diced mango
Take a natural flower petal and place in center
Let it set in the fridge for 8 hours

My Notes: It actually tastes like ice cream, smooth creamy and sweet. But it has no added sugar, naturally made and is high in protein a perfect dessert when on a diet cut. The natural mango pulp contributes to its natural sweetness and tanginess. The diced mango are fun to chew on every bite. The flower petal in the middle is my signature move take it out before eating just for aethetics.

Alternatives:
Can use store mango juice 
Can add dry fruits such as raisins, almonds, cashews and pistachios
