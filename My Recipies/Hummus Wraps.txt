Name:  Hummus Wraps 
Cuisine: Wrap 
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 30 mins 
Cook TIme: 25 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Spicy, Garlicky
Number of Wraps: 2 

Ingredients:
Multigrain Tortilla Wrap
Chicken Breast 100g
Onions 1 piece
Lettuce leaves 2 big
Garlic Cloves 3-4 
Tomatoes 1 piece 
Green Capsicum ¼ piece
Yogurt ½ cup
Lemon ½ piece
Italian Herbs
My Quick Hummus 3 tbsp
Potato 1 
Cheese Slices 2

Procedure:
In a bown add yogurt, garlic, lemon, italian herbs and pepper mix well
Then add chopped chicken breast and let it marrinate for 20 mins
Chop Potato into wedges, add little oil, salt and italian herbs and bake them in oven for 20 mins at 200 degrees
Add the chicken marinate in a pan cook till all pieces are brown and cooked
Light toast tortilla wrap, then apply hummus
Add lettuce leaf, cube cut veggies, chicken pieces and potato wedges
Wrap it up and toast it for little longer till the wrap is brown and toasty
Serve with remaining Wedges 

My Notes: The wrap is filling due to chicken and potatoes, it is earthy and herby. The veggies makes it supper fresh especially the lettuce
 
Alternatives:
Can use more veggies 
Can use different meat
Can use Store hummus 
Can use traditional Hummus
Can use different marinate
