Name: Traditional Hummus
Cuisine: Lebanese
Type: Dip
Prep Time: 8 hours (soaking Chole/Chickpeas) + 10 mins
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 2/5
Savoury Level: 4/5
Salt Level: 4/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 4/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from sesame seeds
Main Flavour profile: Rich Earthy flavour and Garlic dominant

Ingredients:
Chole/Chickpeas 1 Cup
Garlic Cloves 4-5
Sesme Seeds ½ cup
Salt 
Olive Oil 4-8 tbsp
Lemon ½ 
Red Chilli powder 1 tsp

Procedure:
Tahini:
Pan roast sesame seeds till golden
Add to mixer with 2-4 tbsp of olive oil
Add pinch of salt and blend till creamy

Boil the chole in a pressure cooker
Take the boiled chole and add it into nutri blender
Add in Garlic cloves, 2-4 tbsp olive oid, tahini, pinch of salt
Mix till smooth
Store in jar, drizzle ½ tbsp of olive oil and sprinkle the red chillie

My Notes: This is a traditional hummus recipe which I find slightly time consuming due to the tahini step. Adding in tahini brings out sweetness in the hummus which I dont mind but can do without it. At the same time tahini makes it extremely rich in tast and earthy which I do not mind at all. I add more garlic as I like the dominant taste of it.

Alternatives:
No Tahini then my alternative Hummus recipe

Additonal:
Add in beatroot to make purple hummus, increasing the freshness and giving it a vibrant purple and a natural sweetness 
Add in spinach to make a green hummus
Add in roasted red capsicum to make red/orange hummus
