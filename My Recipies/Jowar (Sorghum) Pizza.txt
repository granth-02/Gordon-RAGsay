Name: Jowar (Sorghum) Pizza
Cuisine: Indo Italian
Type: Snack
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from Jowar
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, Italian flavour
Serves: 1 

Ingredients:
Onion ¼ piece
Brocoli Floret 1
Corn few kernels
Capsicum ¼ piece
Mozzeralla cheese  2 tbsp 
Jowar Roti 2
Itallian herbs 1 tps
Homemade Marrinara 2 tbsp
Paneer 50g

Procedure:
The veggies should be Jullien
Apply the marinara sauce to both jowar rotis
Spread cheese
Add the veggies and paneer
Air fry it for 10 minutes 

My Notes: This is high protein meal that I make for myself, it is filling at the same time has low calories and does not make me feel bloated. The base being jowar is way lighter on my stomach at the same time my homemade marinara brings that strong italian flavour. The cheese is just melted enough and the veggies bring out the freshness

Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Small Chichken breast pieces can be used (50 grams) instead of paneer
Can cook it on a pan instead of air fryer
Double Quantities to make more wraps
