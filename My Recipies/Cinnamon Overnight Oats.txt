Name: Cinnamon Overnight Oats
Cuisine: Versatile 
Type: Breakfast
Prep Time: 5mins
Cook Time: Overnight/ 8 Hours
Spice Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 3/5
Sweetness Type: Natural
Main Flavour Profile: Cinnamon and Honey
Serves: 1

Ingrediants:
Rolled Oats 90g
Skimmed Milk 150ml
Powdered Cinnamon 1/2 Teaspoon
Honey 1 Tablespoon
Chopped Almonds 5
Chia Seeds 1 Tablespoon
Pumkin seeds 1 Tablespoon
Chocolate chips 1 tablespoon

Procedure:
Add Milk, Honey and Cinnamon into a nutriblender, Blend till all mixed well 
In a bowl add Rolled oats and mixed milk, top it with chia seeds
Put it in the fridge let it soak overnight or 8 hours
Next day top it up with pumpkin seeds and almonds and chocolate chips

My Notes: I love the cinnamon flavour being dominant, one tablespoon of honey is more that enough for sweetness, the pumpkin seeds bring saltiness to the dish balancing the sweetness from honey and cinnamon. The small amount of chocolate chips contributes to the crunchy factor along with almonds and give a hint of chocolatey taste. Perfect for a pre-workout meal, I do not like adding banana to the dish as it makes it overtly sweet and the banana is way too dominant. The reason I used skimmed milk/light milk is to save on calories as I dont want to feel bloaty during workout. Also do not prefer walnuts as its bitterness throws the dish off balance

Alternatives:
Raisins instead of Chocolatechips
Pistachips instead of almonds
Sunflower seeds instead of pumpkin seeds
