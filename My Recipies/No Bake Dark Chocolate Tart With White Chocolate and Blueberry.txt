Name:  No Bake Dark Chocolate Tart With White Chocolate and Blueberry
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 40 mins 
Cook TIme: Overnight
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Dark chocolate, white chocolate, blueberry and Whipping cream
Main Flavour profile: Rich Chocolatey, Bitter coco taste, fruity and overall sweet

Ingredients:
Digestive Biscuit 2 packet
55% Dark Chocolate 150g
Whipping Cream ½  cup
Butter 7 tbps
White Chocolate 50g
Bluberry ½ pack
Brown sugar 4 tbsp

Procedure:
Blend biscuit till it is fine
Add in butter and mix well
Add it in the tart tin and press it hard on the base and edges, set it in the fridge for 15 mins
In a different bowl take melted chocolate and mix it with whipping cream, mix it well
Add the filling in the tart tin and let it set for 2 hours
Make a blueberry compote using blueberries and brown sugar
Melt the white chocolate and mix is sparsely with blueberry compote
Add it on the top of tart
Let the tart set overnight 

My Notes: Dense, Smooth ,creamy and deep darkchocolate flavour. The digestive biscuit base is perfect for the dark sweet filling, and the white chocolate blueberry compote brings something completely new to the table. WHite chocolate brings uniqueness to the dish and balances the bitterness of dark chocolate and the blueberry compote brings fruitiness, tanginess and slight acidity to the dish wich combines well with other ingredients.

Alternatives:
Other biscuits can also be used
Use 75% bitter chocolate
Can use strawberry compote instead of blueberry
