Name: Jowar (Sorghum) Mexican Quesadilla
Cuisine: Indo-Mexican
Type: Snack
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from Jowar
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, mexican flavour
Serves: 1 


Ingredients:
Onion ¼ piece
Tomoatoes ¼ piece
Corn few kernels
Capsicum ¼ piece
Brocoli 1 floret
Mozzeralla cheese  2 tbsp 
Jowar Roti 2
Yogurt 2 tbsp
Peri Peri powder 1 tbsp
Paneer 50g

Procedure:
Dice the veggies 
Mix yogurt and Peri Peri powder till combined and spread that on both jowar roties
On one of the rotis add cheese, veggies and paneer
Put the other roti on the top of it 
Air fry it for 9 minutes 

My Notes: This is high protein meal that I make for myself, it is filling at the same time has low calories and does not make me feel bloated. The low calorie, high protein Peri Peri dip is the dominant flavour gives it an Mexican spicy taste. The veggies are added to make it fresh, and one cheese slice it more than enough cheese as I like it but I do not like over the top cheesy taste. 

Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Small Chichken breast pieces can be used (50 grams) instead of paneer
Can cook it on a pan instead of air fryer
Double Quantities to make more wraps
