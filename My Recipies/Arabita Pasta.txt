Name: Arabita Pasta
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From the cheese and tomatoes
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Pasta - Fusilli
Onion 1.5 piece
Brocoli 5 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Mozzeralla cheese  2 tbsp 
Baby corn 3 pieces
Basil leaves 4-5 
Cherry tomato 1 piece
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Homemade Marrinara ½ cup
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Parmassaen cheese powder

Procedure:
Boil Pasta for 8 minutes 
The veggies should be Jullien
In a pan add olive oil then add garlic, italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add onions and once it is golden add in the marinara sauce and coat well
Add the rest veggies saute till cooked
Add in the pasta and mix well
Add pasta water to balance the dish
Serve in a pasta dish garnish with fresh basil and cherry tomato, lastly sprinkle parmesan cheese

My Notes: I prefer my pasta to be on a saucy side and really herby. I love adding more vegetable that pasta as it increases the volume at the same time brings freshness. The Marinarra sauce is the main carrie of flavour, the cherry tomato is actually my signature move, alway top it in the center with to basil on its top.

Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Can use anyother style of pasta (eg - Penne, Farfalle and etc)
Ready made marinara sauce instead of homemade to save time, but add salt, herbs to make it more flavourfull
Additonal:
To add protein bake one chicken brest, the brest should be coated in olive oil and italian herbs, added to the final pasta dish and mixed well
