Name: Quick Hummus
Cuisine: Lebanese
Type: Dip
Prep Time: 8 hours (soaking Chole/Chickpeas)
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 4/5
Salt Level: 4/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 4/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from chole
Main Flavour profile: Rich Earthy flavour and Garlic dominant

Ingredients:
Chole/Chickpeas 1 Cup
Garlic Cloves 4-5
Oregano 1-1.5 tbsp
Chilli Flakes 1-1.5 tbsp
Salt 
Olive Oil 4-8 tbsp
Lemon ½ 

Procedure:
Boil the chole in a pressure cooker
Take the boiled chole and add it into nutri blender
Add in Garlic cloves, 2-4 tbsp olive oid, oregano and chilli flakes, pinch of salt
Might need to add water 2-4 tbsp 
Mix till smooth
Store in jar, drizzle ½ tbsp of olive oil 

My Notes: This is my quick hummus, infact preferred hummus recipe as it is more savoury and garlic, olive oil naturally shine through. Adding in the oregano can make it more italian but as a matter of fact the earth taste of chickpeas and garlic is so strong that the oregano does nothing but rather just alleviates their flavour. The only possible flag is that the creaminess is not as good as traditional hummus so to counter that I add in a little water to make it more smoother. The Chilli flakes help in additional spice

Additonal:
Add Chopped Olives to make it richer
