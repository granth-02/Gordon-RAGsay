Name:  Marinara Sandwich
Cuisine: Sandwich
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 3/5 
Sweetness type: From Marinara 
Main Flavour profile: Spicy, Tangy, Italian Flavours 
Number of sandwiches: 2

Ingredients:
Sour Dough
Homemade marinara sauce 4 tbsp
Onion 1 piece
Yellow Capsicum ¼ piece
Green Capsicum ¼ piece
Brocoli 2 Florets chopped 
Garlic cloves 2-4 cloves
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Garlic Cloves 2
Italian Herbs 2 tbsp 
Mozzerlla Cheese 2 tbsp

Procedure:
In a bowl add my tandoori dip and marinate the paneer and coriander for 15 minutes 
Add olive oil, garlic and italian herbs to a pan
Saute till garlic is golden-brown
Add in the veggies and sauter well
Chop 4 slices from the sour dough
Light toast them on pan
Spread marinara sauce on them
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese in the base and add the veggies
Close the the sandwich and toast till bread is brown

My Notes: Here the main aim is to serve a pizza as a sandwich, it is rich in italian flavour tangy herby and spicy due to marinara. The fresh sauted veggies keep everything light and elevates the sandwich.
 
Alternatives:
Can add chicken breast as a filling
Can add minced beef as a filling
Can use ready made/store marinara/pasta sauce
