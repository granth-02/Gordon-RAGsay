Name:  Hummus Sandwich
Cuisine: Sandwich
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 4/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 3/5 
Sweetness type: Hummus and cheese
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Garlicky, Herby full of veggies
Number of sandwiches: 2

Ingredients:
Sour Dough
My Quick Hummus 5 tbsp
Onion 1 piece
Green Capsicum 1 piece
Tomatoes 1 piece
Boiled Brocoli 2 florets chopped
Lettuce leaves 2 big pieces
Olive oil 3 tbsp mixed with italian herbs
Cheese Slices 2

Procedure:
Chop 4 slices from the sour dough
Light toast them on pan
Spread hummus on all of them, add in cheese slice and veggies
Drissle the olive oil mixed with italian herbs on top before closing sandwich
Close the the sandwich and toast till bread is brown
Serve with side of Hummus

My Notes: Fresh light yet filling sandwich. The hummus as a dip makes the sandwich heavy and filling and also dominates the taste, instead of dipping bread and veggies separately in the hummus I recreate that taste by incorporating it in a sandwich. 
 
Alternatives:
Can add more veggies
Can use chicken breast in the main filling
Can use Traditional hummus recipe 
Can use ready made/store bought hummus
