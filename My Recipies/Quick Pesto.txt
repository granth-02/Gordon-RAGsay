Name: Quick Pesto
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dip
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 1/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 4/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 4/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Rich Earthy flavour and Garlic dominant, Very Herby due to the basil

Ingredients:
Basil leaves 40g
Garlic Cloves 4-5
Olive Oil 4-8 tbsp
Lemon ¼ 
Almonds 5-8 pieces
Greek Yogurt 150ml
salt

Procedure:
Add Basil, olive oil, garlic cloves, salt, almonds lemon squeezed to nutri blender
Blend it till everything is mixed
Add this mixture into Yogurt and mix it well

My Notes: Traditional pesto requires pine nuts/walnuts/almonds in huge quantities to make a creamy and smooth pesto. Here I am achieving the similar taste to the original one but a lot cheaper, higher in protein and less calories. The yogurt brings the creamy and smooth texture along with its tanginess, the few almonds grounds the pesto making it nutty and earthy where basil, olive oil and garlic are the main heroes

Alternatives:
If no almonds then Cashew nuts would also work, even walnuts but I do not like that
