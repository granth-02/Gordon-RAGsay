Name:  Spinach Cottage cheese Raviolli
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 2 Hours 30 mins
Cook TIme: 45 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 4/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 3/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Light cream
Main Flavour profile: Rich herby, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour 2.5 cups
Home made marinara sauce ½ cup
Light Cream / Cooking Cream ¼ cup
Mozzerlla cheese 4 tbsp
Onion 1 piece 
Brocoli 4 Florets
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Spinach leaves 10 pieces
Cottage Cheese 1 cup
Baby corn 3 pieces
Mushroom 5 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Olive oil 5 tbsp

Procedure:
Ravioli Sheets:
Take whole wheat, add it oil, hot water and salt 
Knead well and create 5 round balls
Using a rolling pin and create 5 round sheets, roll till they are translucent
Cover them using cloth and let it rest for 2 hours
Take a small bowl using is cut smaller circles make sure they are even

Sauce:
In a pan add olive oil, garlic and seasoning, saute till golden
Add in the marinara sauce mix well
Add in the light cream and mozzarella cheese mix well

Sauted filling:
All veggies and paneer should be finely diced 
In a non stick pan add olive oil, chopped garlic cloves and italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add in veggies and cottage cheese mix well and saute well

Assembly:
Take one sheet and add in a spoon full of the filling
Apply water with fingers on the edges
Take another sheet and cover it up
Using a for press the edges to seal it up
Continue till all sheets are used, boil all the ravioli for 5-7 mins
In a pasta dish pour in the sauce, take out ravioli and place it
Cover it up with more sauce, top it up with cherry tomato basil and cheese

My Notes: This Ravioli is heavy, cheesy, spicey, packed with flavour. The filling is simplistic the spinach brings the earthiness in the dish which is elevated by garlic and seasoning, the cottage cheese minds perfectly with the spinach giving it a salty taste but not too cheesy. The base sauce is rich in flavour derived from my rose pasta which pairs perfectly with the ravioli. Again using whole wheat as its is an healthier option.
 
Alternatives:
Can add less veggies
Ready made marinara sauce instead of homemade to save time, but add salt, herbs to make it more flavourfull
Can use all purpose flour too but prefer whole wheat
Can use chicken/beef instead 
Ready made/ store pasta sheets
