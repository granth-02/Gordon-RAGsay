Name:  Paneer Salad 
Cuisine: Salad
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Spicy, Garlicky, herby

Ingredients:
Paneer 100g
Onions 1 piece
Lettuce leaves 2 big
Garlic Cloves 3-4 
Tomatoes 1 piece 
Green Capsicum ½ piece
Brocoli 5-6 Florets
Mushroom 5-6 pieces
Italian Herbs
My Quick Hummus 3 tbsp
Olive oil 3 tbsp

Procedure:
In a pot add olive oil garlic and saute till its golden
Add in all veggies and sauter well
Air fry paneer for 8 mins
In a small bowl add grated garlic, lemon juice and italian herbs mix well 
Then add the mixture to the salad and mix well
Add paneer and mix well

My Notes: High protein full of fresh veggies herby spicy salad. Can use different dips here too like pesto, hummus, tandoori dip
 
Alternatives:
Can use more veggies 
Can use different meat
Can use ready made/Store bought dips as a main flavour additive
