Name: Tandoori Dip
Cuisine: Indian 
Type: Dip
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from yogurt
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, Garlic, tangy  flavour 


Ingredients:
Yogurt 1 cup
Indian Spices (Red Chilli powder 2 teaspoon, turmeric 1 teaspoon, teaspoon, Coriander Powder 2 teaspoons)
Peri Peri powder 1 tbsp
Garlic Cloves 3-4
Lemon ½ 

Procedure:
Add all the ingredients in one bowl and mix will

My Notes: Tandoori dip usually comes with mayo which is fatty and too sweet for my liking, instead I like to use yogurt which his high protein and lighter. The Indian spices gives the high amount of flavour and spice levels that I love.

Alternatives:
Curd or Dahi can be used instead of Yogurt
