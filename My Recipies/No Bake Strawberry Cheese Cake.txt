Name:  No Bake Strawberry Cheese Cake
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 30 mins 
Cook TIme: Overnight
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 1/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Strawberry and Whipping cream
Main Flavour profile: Rich, Creamy, fruity, slightly tangy taste overall sweet

Ingredients:
Digestive Biscuit 1 packet
Strawberries 1 pack
Brown sugar 4 tbps
Whipping Cream ½  cup
Cream cheese 250g
Butter 7 tbps

Procedure:
Crumble the biscuit into a blender till its is fine 
Add it into a bowl and add melted butter
Add the biscuit into a cake tin and press it hard using a spoon to make it a solid base. Refrigerate for 15 mins
In a different bowl empty creamcheese and soften it a bit In a separate bowl beat the whipping cream to medium-high peaks
Add the whipping cream to the creamcheese base and fold it slowly also add diced up strawberries, mix well
Transfer the filling to the cake tin and let it set for 1 hour
Make Strawberry compote using strawberries and brown sugar
Pour the compote on top 
Now let the entire cake set overnight

My Notes: Creamy, sweet, fruity, dense and rich in flavour. The best part is that it as no extra added sugar as I like my desserts to be at the lower end of sweetness.. The main flavour profile is strawberries, it pairs perfectly with whipping cream and cream cheese. The tine chunks in the main base lifts the dist to a new level. The compote does have added sugar but it is very less than what is usually recommended

Alternatives:
Can use ready made strawberry sauce but wont recommend
