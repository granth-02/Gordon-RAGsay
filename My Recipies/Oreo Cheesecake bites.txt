Name:  Oreo Cheesecake bites
Cuisine: Sweet Bites
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 20 mins 
Cook TIme: 2 hours
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Dark chocolate, oreo 
Main Flavour profile: Decedant, dark chocolate, dense ,gooey, creamy, oreo

Ingredients:
Oreo 2 packets
Dark chocolate 75% 150g
Cream cheese 150g
Butter 7 tbsp

Procedure:
Blend one packet of oreo ensure it is medium corse to fine
Add in butter, combine well, let it set for 15 mins
In a cake tin add press the blended oreo in the base using a spoon
For main filling, mix cream cheese with melted chocolate with ¼ pack of blended oreos
Mix well and add it on top of the base
Take ¾ of oreo packt and using a rolling pin crush them coarsely
Top the filling up with haphazardly crushed oreos
Let it set for 2 hours
Cut into squares

My Notes: Super fudgy, decedant, creamy oreo cheesecake bites. It is super chocolatey but not too sweet as the base is bitter chocolate (75%). The corese oreo bits are perfect for the smooth and creamy filling.

Alternatives:
Can also add crushed chocolate waffle cones
