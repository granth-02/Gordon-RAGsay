Name:  No Bake Biscoff Cheese Cake
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 30 mins 
Cook TIme: Overnight
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 1/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Biscoff and Whipping cream
Main Flavour profile: Sweet, slight saltiness, biscoff

Ingredients:
Biscoff Biscuit 1 packet
Biscoff Spread ½ Jar
Whipping Cream ¼ cup
Cream cheese 250g
Butter 7 tbps

Procedure:
Crumble the biscuit into a blender till its is fine 
Add it into a bowl and add melted butter
Add the biscuit into a cake tin and press it hard using a spoon to make it a solid base. Refrigerate for 15 mins
In a different bowl empty creamcheese and soften it a bit, add Biscoff spread and mix it well
In a separate bowl beat the whipping cream to medium-high peaks
Add the whipping cream to the creamcheese base and fold it slowly, mix well
Transfer the filling to the cake tin and let it set for 1 hour
Melt remaining biscoff spread and pour it over the filling
Now let the entire cake set overnight

My Notes: Creamy, sweet, cheese, dense and rich in flavour. The best part is that it as no extra added sugar as I like my desserts to be at the lower end of sweetness. Biscoff biscuit gives out rich carmelised butter taste the crunchy base that is, the center filling is smooth, creamy and sweet topped with pure melted biscoff spread creating the perfect dessert.
 
Alternatives:
None, the recipe is full proof
