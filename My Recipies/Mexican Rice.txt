Name:  Mexican Rice
Cuisine: Mexican
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 30 mins (8 hours for soaking kidney beans)
Cook TIme: 45 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 4/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Cheese and tomatoes
Main Flavour profile: Spicy, Tangy and mexican flavour

Ingredients:
Soaked kidney beans ½ cup
Nachos 2 bags
Rice 1 Cup
Tomatoes 2 pieces 
Onions 3 pieces
Corriander leaves handful 
Green Capsicum 1 piece
Brocoli 5 Floret 
Corn Kernels ¼ cup
Whole wheat flour ¼ cup 
Peri Peri powder ¼ cup
Garlic Cloves 6-7 
Lemon ½ piece
Milk 1 cup
Pepper to taste
Cheese Slice 4

Procedure:
Salsa:-
Take two tomatoes and onions and finely dice them
Add in salt, Lemon juice and coriander leaves 
Mix Well

Cheese Sauce:-
Add milk to a pan and bring it to a simmer
Add Cheese slices ensure it is melted
Add whole wheat flower and ensure there are no lumps
Keep on adding milk to time to time to ensure consistency
Add in peri peri powder 

Rice:-
Add olive oil to the base, add in chopped garlic and peri peri powder, pepper, saute till golden brown
Add in kidney beans (boiled) mix well 
Add all the veggies and saute till cooked
Add in the rice and mix well
*Add more peri peri powder if required*

Assembly:-
Take a bowl add salsa and cheese sauce to the base
Add little rice and top it with salsa cheese sauce and crumbled nachos
Repeat till bowl full, on the last layer add two whole nacho chips
Serve Bowl with extra nachos, salsa and cheese sauce on the side

My Notes: This is a hearty meal, it is spicy, tangy and full on flavour town. I love adding extra veggies to elevate the dish. The Rice as a base is spicy, the salsa is tangy with a zing and the creamy cheese sauce balances out the entire dish
 
Alternatives:
Can add more veggies 
Canned kidney beans if dont have regular beans 
Ready made/store Cheese sauce if dont want to make homemade sauce
Ready made/store salsa dip if dont want to make homemade salsa 
Can also add meat like chicken in the dish
