Name: Quick Pesto Pasta
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From the cheese and yogurt
Main Flavour profile: Rich herby, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Pasta - Spaghetti 
Onion 1.5 piece
Brocoli 5 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Mozzeralla cheese  4 tbsp 
Baby corn 3 pieces
Mushroom 4 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Basil leaves 4-5 
Cherry tomato 1 piece
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Olive oil 2 tbsp
My Quick Pesto 4-5 tbsp

Procedure:
Boil Pasta for 8 minutes 
The veggies should be Jullien
In a pan add olive oil then add garlic, italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add the rest veggies saute till cooked
Add in the my quick pesto along with mozzarella cheese and mix well
Add in the pasta and mix well
Add pasta water to balance the dish
Serve in a pasta dish garnish with fresh basil and cherry tomato

My Notes: I prefer my pasta to be on a saucy side and really herby. I love adding more vegetable that pasta as it increases the volume at the same time brings freshness. The quick pesto I make using yogurt really makes this dish simple. Cheap and high protein. It still holds the dominant basil pesto taste, garlic and italian herbs helps in elevating it.
 
Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Can use anyother time of Pasta (Fettucini, and etc)
Can make traditional pesto too
Can use ready made/store bought pesto
To add protein bake one chicken brest, the brest should be coated in olive oil and italian herbs, added to the final pasta dish and mixed well
