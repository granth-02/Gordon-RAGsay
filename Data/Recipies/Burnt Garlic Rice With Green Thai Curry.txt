Name:  Burnt Garlic Rice With Green Thai Curry
Cuisine: Thai
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 30 mins 
Cook TIme: 20 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 4/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Coconut Milk
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Tangy, Sweet and Thai flavour

Ingredients:
Lemon Grass 1-3 pieces
Ginger 1-2 pieces
Spring Onion 1-3 pieces
Green chilli 2-4 pieces
Garlic Cloves 4-5 pieces
Zuccini 2 pieces
Cherry Tomatoes 4-5 piece
Onion 1-1.5 piece
Brocoli 4-5 Florets
Baby Corn 4-5 pieces
Mushroom 3-4 pieces
Yellow Capsicum ¼ piece
Red Capsicum ¼ piece
Coconut Milk 2-3 Cans
Rice 2 Cups 
Lemon Juice 2 tbsp

Procedure:
Green Curry Paste:-
Finely chop lemon grass, 2-3 garlic cloves, ginger, green chillie and spring onions
Add them into nutri blunder, add in salt and lemon juice 
Blend till a paste

Curry:-
The vegetables should be large, juliane cut, huge chunks
First saute it till cooked, should be like al dante 
Keep them aside in dish, in the same pan add in the curry paste
Slowly at low-med gass add in the coconut milk
Properly mix the coconut milk and curry paste
Add the sauted veggies ensuring everything is coated
Bunrt Garlic Rice:-
Take 3-5 chopped garlic cloves
On medium heat add cloves into a pan with oil
Let the garlic become brown - dark brown
Add in cooked rice and mix well ensuring Garlic is coated 

My Notes: This is a hearty meal, it is sweet, tangy, earthy and full on flavour town. The main reason behind keeping huge chunk of veggies is that it balances the entire curry, ensuring burst of freshness in every bite. Even though I do not like sweet things usually this is a huge exception mostly because it is also slightly spicey, full of flavour and perfectly savoury. The burnt garlic rice is what makes the dish a magnum opus. Also here I am using regular ginger due to its easy availability and is cheaper, but traditionally galangal.
 
Alternatives:
Can add more veggies 
Try using galangal instead of ginger 
Also can get ready made/store green curry paste if in a haste
Can also add chicken breast or beef
