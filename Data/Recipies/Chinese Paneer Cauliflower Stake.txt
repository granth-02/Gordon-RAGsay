Name:  Chinese Paneer Cauliflower Stake 
Cuisine: Plant-Based
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins 
Cook TIme: 25 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From soy sauce and honey 
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Nutty, Spicy, Tangy and Asian Flavour

Ingredients:
Cauliflower 1 piece
Paneer 100 grams
Garlic Cloves 2-3 piece
Olive oil 3 tbsp
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Pepper to taste
Red chilli sauce 2 tbsp
Honey 1 tbsp

Procedure:
In a pan add olive oil and garlic, saute till golden
Add in 1.5 tbsp of soya sauce, 1 tsp of red chilli sauce and honey
Add medium sized chunks of paneer and cook well
Cut a slice of cauliflower such a way it’s a huge chunk and flat on both sides
Rub the cauliflower piece with olive oil, soya sauce and red chilli sauce
Add it into an air fryer for 15 minutes, on half way point flip the steak piece
In a serving dish add in the paneer base
Place the steak in the center, drizzle it with little olive oil

My Notes: Super fancy veg dish that I like to serve to impress. It is very simple to make and damn tasty. The dominant taste is of soy, garlic and red chili sauce as it is the main base. The steak it self is a great alternative to meat, and the cauliflower brings great earthy and nutty flavour. Overall it is spicy, tangy, a little sweet just enough to assist the spice level.
 
Alternatives:
Can add veggies like onion, capsicum, spring onion in the base 
Can use Tofu instead of paneer
Can add szechwan sauce to make it more spicy
