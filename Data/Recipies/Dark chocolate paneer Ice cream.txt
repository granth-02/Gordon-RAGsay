Name:  Dark chocolate paneer Ice cream 
Cuisine: Ice Cream
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 8 hours
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Dark chocolate
Main Flavour profile: Rich Chocolatey, Bitter coco taste overall sweet

Ingredients:
Paneer 200g
Milk ¼ cup
Unsweetened coco powder 2 tbsp
Dark chocolate 75g
Honey 2 tbsp
Chocolate chips ¼ cup

Procedure:
Blend paneer, milk, coco powder, melted dark chocolate and honey in nutri blender
Ensure it is completely smooth
Pour it in a jar, and top it with chocolate chips
Let it set in the fridge for 8 hours

My Notes: It actually tastes like ice cream, smooth creamy and sweet. But it has no added sugar, naturally made and is high in protein a perfect dessert when on a diet cut. The coco powder and dark chocolate successfully mask the paneer taste and make it chocolatey, the milk helps in achieving smooth consistency, banger dessert

Alternatives:
Can use Bitter chocolate 75% 
Instead of chocolate chips can add chopped dark chocolate
Can add dry fruits such as almonds
