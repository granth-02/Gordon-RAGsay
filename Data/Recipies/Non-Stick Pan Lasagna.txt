Name:  Non-Stick Pan Lasagna
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 2 Hours 30 mins
Cook TIme: 45 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 3/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Cheese sauce
Main Flavour profile: Rich herby, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour 2.5 cups
Cheese Slice 3 pieces 
Home made marinara sauce ½ cup
Milk 1 - 1.5 cup
Onion 2 piece
Brocoli 8 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Spinach leaves 10 pieces
Mozzeralla Cheese 5 tbsp
Baby corn 3 pieces
Corn kernels ¼ cup
Mushroom 5 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Paneer 100g
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Olive oil 5 tbsp

Procedure:
Lasagna Sheets:
Take whole wheat, add it oil, hot water and salt 
Knead well and create 5 round balls
Using a rolling pin and create 5 round sheets, roll till they are translucent
Cover them using cloth and let it rest for 2 hours

Cheese Sauce:
Add milk into pan and let it simmer a bit
Add in the whole wheat flour using a whisk mix well till its not lumpy
Add in cheese slice and mix well
Add in italian herbs

Sauted filling:
All veggies and paneer should be finely diced 
In a non stick pan add olive oil, chopped garlic cloves and italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add in veggies and paneer mix well and saute well

Assembly:
Take a deep cooking pot apply olive oil at the bottom
One scoop of marinara sauce at the bottom
Add in One sheet, on it apply marinara sauce, filling and cheese sauce and sprinkle a little mozzarella
Repeat the step till last sheet, on the last sheet add remaining stuffing, mozzarella and cheese sauce. Here add generous amount of mozzarella cheese
Cover the pan with a transparent lid and cook it on medium heat for 45 mins
After cooking let it cool, sprinkle herbs slice it and serve 

My Notes: This lasagna is heavy, cheesy, spicey, packed with flavour. The paneer brings in the protein, the high amount of veggies helps in filling the tummy, the mozzarella cheese in every slice compliments every flavour. Here the pasta sheet is made without using egg and all purpose flour with an healthier wholewheat flour which also brings natural sweetness in the dish and its not heavy on stomach.
 
Alternatives:
Can add more veggies
Can make square or rectangle pasta sheets
Can use traditional oven in order to cook it traditionally
Ready made marinara sauce instead of homemade to save time, but add salt, herbs to make it more flavourfull
Can use all purpose flour too but prefer whole wheat
Can use chicken/beef instead of paneer
Ready made/ store pasta sheets
