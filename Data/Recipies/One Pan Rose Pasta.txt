Name: One Pan Rose Pasta
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From the cheese and tomatoes
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Pasta - Penne
Onion 1.5 piece
Brocoli 5 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Mozzeralla cheese  2 tbsp 
Baby corn 3 pieces
Mushroom 4 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Basil leaves 4-5 
Cherry tomato 1 piece
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Homemade Marrinara ½ cup
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Cooking/Light Cream 100ml

Procedure:
Boil Pasta for 8 minutes 
The veggies should be Jullien
In a pan add olive oil then add garlic, italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add onions and once it is golden add in the marinara sauce and coat well
Add the rest veggies saute till cooked
Add in the light cream along with mozzarella cheese and mix well till sauce is pink/orange
Add in the pasta and mix well
Add pasta water to balance the dish
Serve in a pasta dish garnish with fresh basil and cherry tomato

My Notes: I prefer my pasta to be on a saucy side and really herby. I love adding more vegetable that pasta as it increases the volume at the same time brings freshness. In a rose one has to make different white sauce but here I am skipping it as it takes more time and money, and just add light cream and mozzarella cheese, when they combine well it essentially does the job perfectly, even better for me as it is nor very cheesy at the same time has the flavour and perfect creaminess.
Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Can use anyother time of Pasta
Ready made marinara sauce instead of homemade to save time, but add salt, herbs to make it more flavourfull
To add protein bake one chicken brest, the brest should be coated in olive oil and italian herbs, added to the final pasta dish and mixed well
Chooped sausages
Meat balls
