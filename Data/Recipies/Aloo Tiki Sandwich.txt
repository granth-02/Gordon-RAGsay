Name:  Aloo Tiki Sandwich
Cuisine: Sandwich
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From yogurt and potato  
Main Flavour profile: Spicy, Tangy, Indian spices
Number of sandwiches: 2

Ingredients:
Sour Dough
Yogurt 1 Cup
Corriander ¼ cup
Potato 2 pieces
Onion 1
Garlic cloves 2-4 cloves
Indian Spices (Red Chilli Powder 1.5 tsp, Turmeric 0.5 tsp, Chat masala 1 tsp)
Lemon ½ piece 
Cheese Slices 2

Procedure:
Peel and boil potatoes
Add oil, garlic to a pan
Saute till garlic is golden-brown
Add half mood onion slice and saute till it is light brown 
Add Spice and mix well
Add large chopped pieces of potatoes and mix well
In a bown add yogurt, lemon juice and coriander mix well
Chop 4 slices from the sour dough
Light toast them on pan, then apply yogurt dip on them
Add cheese slices and potato filing
Close the the sandwich and toast till bread is brown
Serve along side yogurt

My Notes: Proper indian sandwich, it is really heavy and filling due to potatoes. The yogurt spread brings out the freshness and introduces moisture in the sandwich or else it would taste very dry. The filling is packed with indian spices and is really spicy and garlicky which is balanced by the yogurt dip. The reason behind sour dough is that it is really light on the tummy and brings out good taste
 
Alternatives:
Can add veggies like capsicum, spring onion in the filling
Can add szechwan sauce to make it more spicy
