Name:  My Ramen Bowl
Cuisine: Asian 
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Really spicy, hot and asian flavours

Ingredients:
Nongshim Shin Ramen 1 Packet
Paneer 100 grams
Spring Onions (greens)
Onion ½ piece
Corn ¼ cup
Brocoli 4 florets
Cheese Slice 1

Procedure:
In a pot prepare shin ramen, along with noodles also add corn and broccoli to boil
Once cooked add in cheese slice
In a bowl toss paneer with oil, salt, red chilli and little turmeric
Air fry the paneer for 10 mins
Take onion slices and air fry them for 8 mins
Serve ramen in a bowl and top it with paneer, sprinkle green onions and air fried onions

My Notes: Super spicy soupy ramen, I dont do much here but I add extra veggies as I prefer that. Also I add in extra protein to complete the meal, the addition of cheese makes the bowl more creamy than proper soupy
 
Alternatives:
Can use more veggies 
Can use chicken, pork or different meat instead of paneer 
Can use tofu instead of paneer 
Can use different shin ramen packet
Can cook paneer on pan if not air fryer
Can Bake paneer in oven
