Name:  Spinach Besan Egg Curry
Cuisine: Indian Egg
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Bitter, Garlicky, Indian spices

Ingredients:
Boiled Eggs 4
Spinach 2 Bunches
Garlic Cloves 3-4 
Onions 1 piece 
Red Capsicum ¼ piece
Carrot ½ piece
Pepper according to taste
Indian Spices (Red chilli 2 tsp, Coriander powder 1 tsp)
Gram flour 6 tbsp

Procedure:
In a pan add garlic and oil
Saute till garlic is brown 
Add in spinach and saute till it releaser water
Add in a little water and mix, then add Gram flour to make it thick
Add water to maintain consistency
Add in cube cut veggies
Add in Egg slices
Serve with Jowar Roti or Rice 

My Notes: A hearty winter time egg meal inspired from my hometown surat. Here the besan/gram flower gives a thick consistency and a nutty taste. The high garlic flavour is prevalent and suits the bitterness of the spinach. Onions and capsicums are for freshness. Overall it is spicy but not that much 
 
Alternatives:
Can use more veggies 
Can use tomatoes instead of spinach to make a red egg curry
