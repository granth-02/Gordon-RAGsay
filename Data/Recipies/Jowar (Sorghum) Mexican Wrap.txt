Name: Jowar (Sorghum) Mexican Wrap
Cuisine: Indo-Mex
Type: Snack
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 10 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from Jowar
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, mexican flavour
Serves: 1 


Ingredients:
Onion ¼ piece
Tomoatoes ¼ piece
Corn few kernels
Capsicum ¼ piece
Cheese Slice 1 
Jowar Roti 1
Yogurt 2 tbsp
Peri Peri powder 1 tbsp
Boiled Egg whites 2

Procedure:
Cut the veggies in Julliene
Mix yogurt and Peri Peri powder till combined and spread that on the jowar roti
Add cheese slice
Add veggies and Egg (also chopped)
Wrap it and air fry it for 7 minutes 

My Notes: This is high protein meal that I make for myself, it is filling at the same time has low calories and does not make me feel bloated. The low calorie, high protein Peri Peri dip is the dominant flavour gives it an Mexican spicy taste. The veggies are added to make it fresh, and one cheese slice it more than enough cheese as I like it but I do not like over the top cheesy taste. 

Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
2 tbsp Mozzerla Cheese instead of Cheese Slice
Paneer can be used instead of eggs
Small Chichken breast pieces can be used (50 grams) instead of eggs
Can cook it on a pan instead of air fryer
Double Quantities to make more wraps
