Name:  Paneer Tikka Sandwich
Cuisine: Sandwich
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 25 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From yogurt 
Main Flavour profile: Spicy, Tangy, Indian spices
Number of sandwiches: 2

Ingredients:
Sour Dough
Yogurt 1 Cup
Corriander ¼ cup
Garlic cloves 2-4 cloves
My Tandoori Dip
Lemon ½ piece 
Cheese Slices 2

Procedure:
In a bowl add my tandoori dip and marinate the paneer and coriander for 15 minutes 
Add oil, garlic to a pan
Saute till garlic is golden-brown
Add the paneer marinate and cook till paneer is crispy and golden brown
Chop 4 slices from the sour dough
Light toast them on pan
Add cheese slices and potato filing
Close the the sandwich and toast till bread is brown
Serve with tandoori dip

My Notes: Proper tikka sandwich, it is really heavy and filling due to the paneer. The tandoori dip used as a marinate makes the filling creamy ensure it doesnt feel dry. The filling is packed with indian spices and is really spicy and garlicky which is balanced by the natural light sweetness of the dip. The reason behind sour dough is that it is really light on the tummy and brings out good taste
 
Alternatives:
Can add veggies like capsicum, onion and tomatoes in the filling
Can use chicken breast instead of paneer to make chicken tikka sandwich
Can use ready made/store tandoori sauce and mix it with yogurt to creat the dip
