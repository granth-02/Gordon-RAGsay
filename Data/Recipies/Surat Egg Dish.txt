Name:  Surat Egg Dish
Cuisine: Indian Egg
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 2/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Tomatoes 
Main Flavour profile: Earthy, Bitter, Garlicky, Indian spices

Ingredients:
Boiled Eggs 4
Garlic Cloves 3-4 
Onions 2 piece 
Tomatoes 2 pieces
Green Capsicum ¼ piece
Brocoli 3-4 florets
Mushrooms 3-4 pieces 
Carrot ½ piece
Pepper according to taste
Indian Spices (Red chilli 2 tsp, Coriander powder 1 tsp, turmeric powder ½ tsp, Garam Masala ¼ tsp)


Procedure:
In a pan add garlic and oil
Saute till garlic is brown 
Add in onion and saute till brown
Add the spices
Add Tomatoes and mash them together to create some sort of paste
Add water to maintain consistency
Add in cube cut veggies
Grate eggs on top and mix well
Serve with Jowar Roti or Rice 

My Notes: A egg meal inspired from my hometown surat. The base is indian tomato onion gravy packed with spices. The veggies increase volume and freshness, the grated eggs is the signature move that makes it apart from other dishes. The dish is spicy, herby and flavourful
 
Alternatives:
Can use more veggies 
Can add a simple omllette in the base top it with this curry we make and grate processed cheese on top to make it spot on
