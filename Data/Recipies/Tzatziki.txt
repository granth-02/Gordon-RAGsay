Name: Tzatziki
Cuisine: Greek
Type: Dip
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 1/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: Natural from yogurt
Main Flavour profile: Rich Earthy, Garlic, Minty and tangy  flavour 


Ingredients:
Yogurt 1 cup
Corriander ¼ cup
Mint leaves a few
Garlic Cloves 3-4
Olive Oil 4 tbsp
Lemon ½ 
Cucumber 1 piece 

Procedure:
In a bowl add in yogurt, olive oil, lemon, mint leaves, coriander, finely chopped cucumber, chopped garlic and mix well

My Notes: This is a traditional recipe requires Dil which can be hard to find sometimes, also does not have mint in it. I like to add coriander as a alternative as it is more accessible, also mint leaves bring freshness and coolness to the dip which elevates the coolness of cucumber. Olive oil and garlic give strong earthy dense flavours

Alternatives:
Coriander instead of dil
