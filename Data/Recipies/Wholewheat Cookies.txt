Name:  Wholewheat Cookies
Cuisine: Sweet Bites
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 20 mins 
Cook TIme: 30 mins
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Dark chocolate, Cookie
Main Flavour profile: Decedant, dark chocolate, dense and gooey

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour 1 cup
55% Dark Chocolate 80g
Butter 100g
Chocolate chips ½ cup
Unsweetened Coco powder 2 tbsp
Milk ¼ Cup
Caster sugar ¼ cup
Brown sugar ¼ cup
Half beaten egg liquid
Vannila extract 1 tsp
Baking soda  ½ tsp

Procedure:
Take the butter, add in both sugars and beat them till mixed well
Take half an egg liquid and add in the mixture, mix well
Add 1 tsp vanilla extract and mix well
Sieve the dry ingredients (whole wheat flour, cooc powder, baking soda) into the mixture 
Combine well, add dark chocolate chunks and roll into balls
Before baking add few chocolate chips and chunks on the top
Bake at 170 degrees for 15 mins

My Notes: Super fudgy, decedant cookies. Wholewheat is naturally sweet and elevates the cookies, I like to add less sugar than required as the sweetness of chocolate chunks are more than enough. They also make the cookies super gooey and fudgy and the extra unsweetened coco powder gives it a bitter taste and slightly browner look.

Alternatives:
Can add other toppings, M&Ms for example
