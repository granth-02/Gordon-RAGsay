Name:  Wholewheat Brownies
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 20 mins 
Cook TIme: 30 mins
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Dark chocolate
Main Flavour profile: Decedant, dark chocolate, dense and gooey

Ingredients:
Whole wheat flour 1 cup
55% Dark Chocolate 150g
Butter ½ cup
Chocolate chips ¼ cup
Brown sugar powdered ¼ cup
Unsweetened Coco powder ¼ cup
Milk ¼ Cup
Baking powder½ tsp
Baking soda  ¼ tsp

Procedure:
Melt the butter and chocolate together using double boiler, mix well till looks velvety
Take a sieve and sieve the flour, coco powder, brown sugar, baking soda and powder together into the melted chocolate 
Mix well, add milk little by little for consistency
Add chopped chocolate chunks
Transfer in baking tin, top it up with move chocolate chunks and chips
Bake in oven for 30 mins at 180

My Notes: Super fudgy, eggles decedant brownies. I like adding chocolate chunks as the melt and make the brownies gooey and fudy, it is dark bitter sweet. Also wholewheat is healthier and naturally sweeter. I add little sugar compared to traditional recipes.

Alternatives:
Can add in toppings like oreo or mnms too
Can add dry fruits like almond and walnuts too
Can add eggs to make it more fudgy
