Name: Quick Aglio Olio
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 4/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 0/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: N/A
Main Flavour profile: Rich herby, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Pasta - Spaghetti 
Onion 1.5 piece
Brocoli 5 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Parmassaen cheese  2 tbsp 
Baby corn 3 pieces
Mushroom 4 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Cherry tomato 1 piece
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Procedure:
Boil Pasta for 8 minutes 
The veggies should be Jullien
In a pan add olive oil then add garlic
Saute till garlic is golden
Add the rest veggies saute till cooked
Add in the pasta and mix well
Add pasta water to balance the dish
Add in Italian herbs and mix well
Serve in a pasta dish garnish with fresh basil, cherry tomato, sprinkle cheese

My Notes: My aglio olio is very different and deviates from the authentic recipe, I like mine to be on high flavour hence I add in italian herbs (oregano and chilli flakes). The pasta water does the heavy lifting when it comes coating the herbs, overall flavour is garlicky and herby
 
Alternatives:
Can add no veggies
To add protein bake one chicken brest, the brest should be coated in olive oil and italian herbs, added to the final pasta dish and mixed well
Sauted Shrimp can be added too
