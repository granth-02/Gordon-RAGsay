Name: White Wine Pasta (Vino Vedura) 
Cuisine: Italian
Type: Dinner
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook TIme: 15 mins
Spice Level: 5/5
Savoury Level: 5/5
Salt Level: 5/5
Sweetness Level: 3/5 
Umami Level: 5/5 
Sweetness type: From the cheese ,tomatoes , Light cream
Main Flavour profile: Rich Spicy, Italian flavour

Ingredients:
Pasta - Spaghetti 
Onion 1.5 piece
Brocoli 5 Florets
Green Capsicum ½   piece
Yellow Capisum ½ piece 
Mozzeralla cheese  4 tbsp 
Baby corn 3 pieces
Mushroom 4 pieces
Olives 3-4 pieces
Basil leaves 4-5 
Cherry tomato 1 piece
Garlic Cloves 4-5 
Itallian herbs 4 tps
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Cooking/Light Cream 200ml
White Wine 1 Glass

Procedure:
Boil Pasta for 8 minutes 
The veggies should be Jullien
In a pan add olive oil then add garlic, italian herbs
Saute till garlic is golden
Add the rest veggies saute till cooked
Add in the light cream along with mozzarella cheese and mix well
Add White Wine
Add in the pasta and mix well
Add pasta water to balance the dish
Serve in a pasta dish garnish with fresh basil and cherry tomato

My Notes: I prefer my pasta to be on a saucy side and really herby. I love adding more vegetable that pasta as it increases the volume at the same time brings freshness. The wine brings a unique taste a hint of sweetness that is. The pasta is creamy and cheesy but not too much as the italian herbs and garlic dominate the dish, making it overall well balanced.
 
Alternatives:
Can swapp any veggies
Can use anyother time of Pasta (Fettucini)
Can use Vodka instead of white wine
Also can skip alcohol 
To add protein bake one chicken brest, the brest should be coated in olive oil and italian herbs, added to the final pasta dish and mixed well
