Name:  No Bake Triple Chocolate Cheese Cake
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 30 mins 
Cook TIme: Overnight
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Chocolate and Whipping cream
Main Flavour profile: Rich Chocolatey, Bitter coco taste, oreo overall sweet

Ingredients:
Oreo Biscuit 1 packet
75% Bitter Chocolate ¾ Bar
55% Dark Chocolate ¾ Bar
Whipping Cream ½  cup
Cream cheese 250g
Butter 7 tbps

Procedure:
Crumble the biscuit into a blender till its is fine 
Add it into a bowl and add melted butter
Add the biscuit into a cake tin and press it hard using a spoon to make it a solid base. Refrigerate for 15 mins
In a different bowl empty creamcheese and soften it a bit, add melted 55% Dark chocolate and mix well
In a separate bowl beat the whipping cream to medium-high peaks
Add the whipping cream to the creamcheese base and fold it slowly, mix well
Transfer the filling to the cake tin and let it set for 1 hour
Melt the 75% bitter chocolate and let it cool a bit, then add remaining whipping cream and mix well.
Pour the bitter chocolate ganache on top 
Now let the entire cake set overnight

My Notes: Creamy, sweet, chocolatey, dense and rich in flavour. The best part is that it as no extra added sugar as I like my desserts to be at the lower end of sweetness. I love bitter chocolate and overall dark chocolates. The main flavour profile is for dark chocolate, the base oreos althoug sweet the biscuit is slightly bitter, then we increase the bitterness of the chocolate in the main filling by adding dark chocolate, and top it with perfect bitterness with bitter chocolate. NOTE I NEVER USE MILK CHOCOLATE AS I DISLIKE IT
 
Alternatives:
Other rich chocolate biscuits would work just fine
Digestive biscuit would also pair well
