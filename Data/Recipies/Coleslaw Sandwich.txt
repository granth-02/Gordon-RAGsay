Name:  Coleslaw Sandwich
Cuisine: Sandwich
Type: Snack / Dinner
Prep Time: 10 mins 
Cook TIme: 5 mins
Spice Level: 3/5
Savoury Level: 3/5
Salt Level: 3/5
Sweetness Level: 4/5 
Umami Level: 3/5 
Sweetness type: From Mayo and Barbecue Sauce 
Main Flavour profile: Mild spicy, Tangy, Sweet
Number of sandwiches: 2

Ingredients:
Bread 4 slices 
Mayo 4 tbsp
Carrots Shredded ¼ cup
Beetroot Shreedded ¼ cup
Cucumber Shredded ¼ cup
Cabbage Shredded ¼ cup
Pepper according to taste
Peri peri powder 2 tsp
Barbacue Sauce 2 tbsp
Butter 2 tbsp

Procedure:
In a bowl add the shredded veggies, mayo, barbecue sauce, salt, pepper, peri peri powder and mix well
Apply butter on all 4 slices of bread
Add filling in the bread and close it, serve

My Notes: Perfect summer eve snack or dinner, fresh, sweet, tangy, smokey and filling. Filled with veggies, the addition of a little peri peri powder gives it a little kick of spice that elevates the entire sandwich, quick and easy to make
 
Alternatives:
Can use different color cabbage
Can use light mayo to save calories
Can add a little sriracha instead of peri peri
Can add red chilli sauce instead of peri peri
