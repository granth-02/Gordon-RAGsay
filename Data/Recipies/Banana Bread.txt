Name:  Banana Bread
Cuisine: Cakes
Type: Dessert 
Prep Time: 20 mins 
Cook TIme: 50 mins
Spice Level: 0/5
Savoury Level: 0/5
Salt Level: 0/5
Sweetness Level: 5/5 
Umami Level: 0/5 
Sweetness type: Banana
Main Flavour profile: Naturally sweet with backend chocolatey taste

Ingredients:
Ripe Bananas 2 pieces
Dark chocolate chunks ¼ cup
Baking soda 1 tsp
Vanilla extract few drops 
Yogurt ⅓ cup
Whole wheat flour 1 cup
Brown sugar 4 tbsp
Almonds 5 chopped 

Procedure:
Mash the ripe bananas thoroughly, add yogurt and vanilla extract and mix well
Add the flour and sugar in the mixture and mix well
Add in dark chocolate chunks
Transfer in cake tinm top it up with more chocolate chunks and almonds
Bake at 180 degrees for 50 mins

My Notes: Natural sweetness from the banana dominates and pairs perfectly dark chocolate as they mitigare the overt sweetness. The yogurt makes the cake super moist and fluffym and it is low sugar and healthy as it uses whole wheat and not all purpose flour.

Alternatives:
Can add more dry fruits
